## 1. 3D print all parts in this [folder](/mechanical_parts/) to the required part number:  
   ![](images/1-%203D%20parts.png)
## 2. Assemble 6 screen mounts:
1. Add 4 m3x6 screws and nuts to each (6) X-brace part:  
   ![](images/2-%20X-brace%20screws.png)
2. Slide makerbeamXL (150mm) on each side of each X-brace:  
   ![](images/3-%20Attach%20beams%20to%20x-brace.png)  
   ![](4-%20beams%20with%20X-brace.png)  
3. Slide in 2 nuts to each side and screw on a printed 32mm (image left) and 34mm (image right) screen bracket to each side as shown:  
   ![](images/5-%20Adding%20screen%20brackets.png)
4. Attach screws and nuts to (12) t-plates and insert into the head of each unit as shown: (flipped)  
   _For TWO of these units, make sure to include two extra nuts between the T-plates_
   ![](images/6-%20Adding%20t-plate.png)
5. Slide another 150mm makerbeamXL on to the top of the unit:    ![](images/8-%20Adding%20top-bar.png)
6. Slide in two nuts and screw on the 17mm screen bracket:  
   ![](images/9-%20Attach%20top%20screen-mount.png)  _Remember the extra nuts for two units_  
    ![](images/Extra%20nuts%20screen%20mount.png)
## 3. Assemble central hub
_Remember to glue in magnets to the top holes so they match those in the fly-base_
1. Add screws and nuts to the central hub object:  
   ![](images/10-%20Central-hub%20screws%20bottom.png)  ![](images/11-%20Central%20hub%20nuts.png)
2. Slide in six makerbeamXL 200mm beams:
   ![](images/12-%20Central%20hub%20attach%20beam.png)  ![](images/13-%20Central%20hub%20with%20all%20beams.png)  
## 4. Assemble the Base
1.  Add screws and nuts to the corner join parts (x6):  ![](images/14-Corner%20join%20attach%20screws.png)
2. Attach a 150mm makerbeam and t-plate (x5): 
	_Don't forget the  nuts shown (left) for two units)
	![](images/15-%20Attach%20beam%20to%20corner%20join%20with%20t-plate.png)
3.  Add two more nuts to the top of the beam and attach the 8mm screen bracket: ![](images/17-%20Attach%20base-unit%20screen%20mount.png)
4.  Insert two extra nuts into TWO sections only:
	![](images/Add%20extra%20nuts.png)
5. Attach the remaining corner part and slot together as shown:
	_Make sure the two sections with extra nuts are OPPOSITE
	Note that these parts aren't yet screwed together_  ![](images/20-%20Partial%20base%20assembly.png)
	
6.  Attach the remaining beam with nuts as shown: ![](images/21-%20Attaching%20final%20beam%20to%20base%20(zoom).png) ![](images/22-%20Attaching%20final%20beam%20to%20base.png) 
7.  Attach remaining t-plates and screws: 
   _Note it's best to keep the screen mounts and t-plates loose enough to slide so they can be adjusted later_![](images/23-%20Completed%20base.png)
8. Attach the central hub ![](images/26-%20Attach%20central%20hub%20to%20base.png)  ![](images/27-%20Screw%20in%20central%20hub.png)

## 7. Assemble the Camera Mount
1. Attach the Camera to the mount
2. Slide the mount on to a 200mm beam
3. Add screws + straight nuts to the corner bracket (x2)
4. Slide in 200mm beams (x2)
