## 2. Assemble 6 screen mounts:
1. Add 4 m3x6 screws and nuts to each (6) X-brace part:  
   ![][images/2-%20X-brace%20screws.png]
2. Slide makerbeamXL (150mm) on each side of each X-brace:  
   !()[images/3- Attach beams to x-brace.png]  
   !()[4- beams with X-brace.png]  
3. Slide in 2 nuts to each side and screw on a printed 32mm (image left) and 34mm (image right) screen bracket to each side as shown:  
   !()[images/5- Adding screen brackets.png]
4. Attach screws and nuts to (12) t-plates and insert into the head of each unit as shown: (flipped)  
   _For TWO of these units, make sure to include two extra nuts between the T-plates_
   !()[images/6- Adding t-plate.png]
5. Slide another 150mm makerbeamXL on to the top of the unit:    ![[images/8- Adding top-bar.png]]
6. Slide in two nuts and screw on the 17mm screen bracket:  
   !()[images/9- Attach top screen-mount.png]  _Remember the extra nuts for two units_  
    !()[Extra nuts screen mount.png]

## 4. Assemble the Base
1.  Add screws and nuts to the corner join parts (x6):  ![[14- Corner join attach screws.png]]
2. Attach a 150mm makerbeam and t-plate (x5): 
	_Don't forget the  nuts shown (left) for two units)
	![[15- Attach beam to corner join with t-plate.png]]
3.  Add two more nuts to the top of the beam and attach the 8mm screen bracket: ![[17- Attach base-unit screen mount.png]]
4.  Insert two extra nuts into TWO sections only:
	![[Add extra nuts.png]]
5. Attach the remaining corner part and slot together as shown:
	_Make sure the two sections with extra nuts are OPPOSITE
	Note that these parts aren't yet screwed together_  ![[20- Partial base assembly.png]]
	
6.  Attach the remaining beam with nuts as shown: ![[21- Attaching final beam to base (zoom).png]] ![[22- Attaching final beam to base.png]] 
7.  Attach remaining t-plates and screws: 
   _Note it's best to keep the screen mounts and t-plates loose enough to slide so they can be adjusted later_![[23- Completed base.png]]
8. Attach the central hub ![[26- Attach central hub to base.png]]  ![[27- Screw in central hub.png]]
